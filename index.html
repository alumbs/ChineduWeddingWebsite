How I met my Wife

Janessa has a very different idea on how we met but I believe we met through a mutual friend of ours and at the time I just thought she was the weirdest child I had ever met. She was quite funny and charming and we easily became friends. Even as friends everyone always knew we would end up together and we have always enjoyed each others company. We were always together getting into crazy adventures like trying to find a Walmart in the middle of the night or trying to walk to a gas station in -40C.



No matter what the adventure was and still is, if she called for my help, I would always be there for her and those were the moments that made me realize I wanted to spend every moment with this girl who I knew I was in love with. It took me quite a while to get the nerve to ask her to even go out with me and even longer to get the nerves to ask her dad for his permission. FYI Daryl is super nice but he scares me. Fortunately for me, he said yes and I was more than delighted to ask the love of my life to spend the rest of her life with me. She said yes and fast-forward 5 months and here we are.
